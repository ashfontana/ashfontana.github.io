CEO Coaching

I intensively coach a small number of CEOs.

Focus

I’m a strategist - not a therapist.

Show ▾ Hide ▾
  • I help CEOs elevate themselves out of the ‘rat race’ of seeking small, incremental improvements in operational effectiveness; I help them walk down the small hills, look around and pick a mountain to climb. I offer external, neutral and objective perspective in making decisions about where and how to focus.
  • Strategically, I help CEOs win through pricing, data, machine learning, research & development, fundraising and competitive positioning.
  • Interpersonally, I help CEOs develop more productive relationships with their investors and board members. I’ve sat on dozens of boards and invested in hundreds of companies in my life.
  • Energetically, I help CEOs maintain very high levels of focus using dozens of methods I’ve developed through decades of bio/psycho/socio-logical experimentation. I’m into cycling, weightlifting and ski touring in a big way, intensely training all year around. I bring a lot of what I learn about performance in these sports into our coaching sessions.

What I don’t do is get into sales & marketing tactics, coach mid-level managers, or coach CEOs through issues that they may have with their direct reports. I’ve found that coaches with a difference background are more helpful in these cases.
